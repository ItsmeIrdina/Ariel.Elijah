Ariel Elijah is a self taught photographer and videographer with a natural talent for visual storytelling. His journey began in high school, where he was actively involved in the media and photography team the very place where his love for the lens blossomed into a lifelong passion.

Known for his versatility, Ariel can capture just about anything, from portraits to landscapes, cinematic videos to event coverage. His skill is not only raw but also refined, thanks to the mentorship of the renowned photographer Rock Empayar, who helped sharpen his artistic eye and professionalism.

Through his work, Ariel continues to grow and inspire others by proving that talent, dedication, and passion are the perfect combination for success.
